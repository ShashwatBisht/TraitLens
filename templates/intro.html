Big Five Personality Test

This personality test is constructed with the "Big-Five Factor Markers" from the International Personality Item Pool (IPIP).

You will be presented with a series of statements about yourself. Please rate each statement according to how accurately it describes you on a scale from 1 (Disagree) to 5 (Agree).

Your responses will be recorded and used for research purposes. The test takes approximately 5-10 minutes to complete.

The test measures five personality dimensions:

  • Extraversion: Sociability, assertiveness, and emotional expressiveness
  • Emotional Stability: Emotional regulation and resilience to stress
  • Agreeableness: Trust, altruism, kindness, and other prosocial behaviors
  • Conscientiousness: Organization, thoroughness, and reliability
  • Openness: Creativity, curiosity, and preference for variety
